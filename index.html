Once again, I spent much of 2017 on the road, traveling and speaking about education technology's histories, ideologies, and mythologies. The Monsters of Education Technology, Part 4 is a collection of about a dozen of those talks on topics ranging from Trump to robots.

It's the fourth book in the "Monsters of Ed-Tech" series – a sequel to my 2014 book The Monsters of Education Technology and to my 2015 book The Revenge of the Monsters of Education Technology and to my 2016 book The Curse of the Monsters of Education Technology.

Publication date: Halloween 2017
